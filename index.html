Interior Painting

We take care of your interior painting needs from ceiling to floors with a sharp eye for details and making it a smooth seamless experience.

Our high quality product include the best products for the job and a meticulous procedure to get beautiful long lasting and profesional results at a reasonable place.

Before painting we prepare walls, ceilings and trim filling nail holes, repairing bad drywall seams, patching holes, fixing cracks and splits. Then, we prime the repaired areas and water stains to stop them from coming back.

Also provide services on wood staining, wallpaper instalation and removal, refinishing and/or painting kitchen cabinets. Staining and/or refinishing wood floors, and much more.

Deck refinish and maintenance

Decks provide us with a relaxing place to enjoy and share with family and friends at home. However, since decks are exposed to the enviroment all year long they need atention.

We do deck repairs, refinishing, painting, staining, handrails, stairs, and patio furniture. We work with wood decks, either pressure treated wood or hardwood, and composite decks.

Wood decks require the most maintenance, they need to be cleaned, light sanded and stained/oiled at least once a year. Composite decks mostly need to be cleaned. We can do all that for you. Just give us a call.
